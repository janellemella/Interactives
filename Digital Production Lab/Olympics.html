By Janelle Mella

What is figure skating?

Figure skaters at the Olympics are placed on the pedestal of their lifetime, performing a mix of long and short programs that showcase their artistry and strength. Skaters are ranked on elements like jumps, spins, creativity, footwork and how well they execute each one, both individually and in pairs. When and where can I watch these skaters?

Figure skating events will begin on Feb. 6 and carry out through Feb. 21. The events will range from Team, ice dance, pair and singles. All events will be availble to stream on Peacock and NBC platforms.
