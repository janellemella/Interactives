Alysa Liu's Comeback

By Janelle Mella

Olympic and World Champion figure skater Alysa Liu will take on her second Olympic games at this year's Milano Cortina Olympics.

She retired after the 2022 Olympics at the age of 16, but has since returned to the ice, but on her own terms.

These are some of her skating accomplishments:

  • Gold in the 2025 Boston World Championships
  • Sixth in the Beijing 2022 Winter Olympics
  • Bronze at the 2022 ISU World Figure Skating Championships
She also came back with freshly dyed hair, which has gained a lot of popularity on social media within the last couple weeks.

This article reported that Alysa Liu will be in this year's Winter games.
